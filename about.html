✦  About Automify

We Automate What
Slows Your Business Down

Automify is an automation agency helping businesses in the UK and Spain eliminate repetitive tasks, save hours every week, and focus on what actually drives growth.

Speak to an Expert  →

We started Automify with a simple belief: business owners should spend their time building, not buried in admin. From a restaurant manager chasing no-show reservations to an e-commerce founder manually syncing orders into a CRM — we saw the same problem everywhere. Too much time wasted on tasks that technology should be handling.


Today we work with businesses across hospitality, retail, services, e-commerce, real estate, and more — across the UK and Spain — delivering real, custom solutions. No templates. No generic setups. Just automation that fits your business like it was built for it. Because it was.
